Get and earn your cards that depict legends, items, animals, spells and actions you can use to duel other players. The game is currently in a work in progress The game is a simple TCG game where you build your deck of cards based on your class. Was made with the help of Adobe Animate There are Three Classes: Warrior, Hunter and Mage Players can play cards that are based on their class, which can attack, defend, cast spells summon creatures, and generally create an advantage for themselves. Some cards have special effects such as causing vulnerability poison, drawing cards...etc Each cards require Action Cost to be used. Players have 5 Action Points per turn and when you run out of Action or Cards you end your turn. The goal of the game is to reduce the opponent’s Health Point to 0. Collaboration is currently underway with this wonderful artist Code_Queenslayer on twitter. They are currently creating high quality character art based on different lootbags, which I thought would be perfect for representing the players in game. Visual Representation Collaboration There are 20 cards* in your Deck, and you draw 5 cards on your turn, * : May be changed for the final release of the game which resets on your next turn.
